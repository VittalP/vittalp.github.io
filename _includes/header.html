Postdoctoral Research Scientist

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at the Johns Hopkins University. I obtained my Ph.D degree in Computer Science from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. I have previously worked as a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at the University of California, Los Angeles. I was also a visiting researcher at the National University of Singapore.

My mission is to "Solve Intelligence". I am currently focussing on the important component of representational learning. To this end, I build deep networks to extract knowledge from the rich, but challenging, data source of images.
